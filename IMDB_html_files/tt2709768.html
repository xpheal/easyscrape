Did You Know?

Trivia

The gag featuring the chestburstser from Alien is the second time it has been used in an Illumination film. The first time was in Despicable Me 2. See more »

Goofs

When Max is trying to save Duke from the cage in the sinking truck, he loses the keys because the key ring is a floating plastic miniature of the Statue of Liberty, so it floats all the way up to the surface water. Snowball then dives from the bridge to help them out: he gets the keys and throws them to Max, which is beneath him, and the keys can be clearly seen sinking towards Max, when they should be floating instead.
