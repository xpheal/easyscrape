User Reviews

 
Pleasantly surprised at the high quality.
5 April 2016 | by Sergio Scout (United States) – See all my reviews

Whenever i watch a new TV show, even if its animation, i keep an open mind. I was pleasantly surprised of the high quality of this show.

Pro: -great voice acting! the voices fit like a glove to the characters. -More or less the same crew of voice actors then with the TV series "Clone wars" which was brilliant in my opinion. -set in a new part of the star wars universe. -the script is so well written that it is even better than most AAA list movies scripts ( with the exception of the marvel movie franchise). -Quite engaging stories that will keep you thinking: "when is the next episode coming?"

Conclusion: it is a must see TV series that does not bore you to tears. There is humor, goofyness (in a good way) and some great action scenes all beautifully interwoven with each other. Personally i do not have any negatives to say about this TV show and that is saying a lot. I will even go as far to say that this TV show is even better than the recent star wars: the force awakens movie ( i had to see it twice, partly because i got free tickets that were about to expire, to actually grasp what that movie was all about.. and quite frankly i fell into the hyping trap of Disney with that one)

Now Disney has had some great movies and TV shows over the years but the star wars the force awakens is a bit ...borderline amateurish to say the least.. and like with many things if you got a crappy script, even AAA+ actors cant make it good.
