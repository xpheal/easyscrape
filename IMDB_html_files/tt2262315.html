User Reviews

 
Super good movie
27 September 2016 | by subxerogravity (United States) – See all my reviews

Two wanna be filmmakers spend a lot of money to do a special social experiment in which they try to convince the grumpy old neighbor across the street that his house is hunted.

Now, the poster is telling me James Caan is going to be that grumpy old man, and if you known anything about James Caan he does a very interesting grumpy old man and had me expecting something completely different.

How this story ended up progressing turned out to be a very intense dramatic experience, with surprisingly a lot of layers to it.

The Good Neighbor was a pleasant surprise, as I was expecting one of those reality camera horror films and got something a little more sophisticated
