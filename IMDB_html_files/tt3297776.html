Storyline

Rick and the others are still prisoners in the boxcar but the time of reckoning has come. They prepare to take on their captors by making weapons from whatever they can find and just wait for them to open the boxcar door. Those in charge however are one step ahead of them. Not far from Terminus Carol, Tyreese and baby Judith hear gunfire and soon after overhear someone talking about Michonne on his walkie talkie. They tie him up and while Tyreese says behind to keep an eye on him, Carol heads off to Terminus to save her friends. Written by garykmcd

A lot happened in this episode than what happened in last season
13 October 2014 | by seshasai-tris (Boston, MA) – See all my reviews

Just finished watching the episode, so writing this review with a walking-dead mind.

the best part of the last season was last sentence spoken by RICK at the end 'They are screwing with the wrong people'.

First of all, the directors of the show are playing with minds of the audience. You define thrill either in pace of the show, killings and live action, set and premise of the show, emotion exchange between character, visual effects and plot etc. Most of the factors have not been met in the last season.

But in this episode pace of the episode, emotion, character, story, violent killings, blood, cool cinematography have been met. They fed what walking dead fans have been craving for the last one year.

This also hints may be the next few episodes are going to lack pace, but that's alright as long as they chip in the other factors mentioned above.

Happy Halloween.
