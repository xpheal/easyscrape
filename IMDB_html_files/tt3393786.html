User Reviews

 
it don't get much worse than this, total let down
22 October 2016 | by WoodBangers Entertainment (United States) – See all my reviews

Where to begin after seeing Jack Reacher: Never Go Back (2016)... For the very first time in a very long time I sit on the edge of my seat trying to avoid walking out of the theater within the first 10 minutes of this movie; all the way till it ended. I was literally the first person to leave the theater when the film stopped, you could see it in everyone and hear it in the theaters of how bad this film was.

I have to hand it to the people who created the trailer of Jack Reacher: Never Go Back, they done such a good job on the trailer that I was excited to go; then I saw the trailer in the first opening moments of the film and released that they changed things up in the trailer to make it look like a better film.

Jack Reacher 2 is not a complete waste of a film, it's good for people who don't notice the stunt doubles during running shots and a fall from the top of a building (4 feet in height). It's a great action film film if you don't notice all the pauses in the scenes to graphically enhance the pose of Tom Cruise face and make up in every still. This film failed beyond short, it's as if they couldn't figure out how to end it, nor figure out how to make the story continue as it would flop from spot to spot. I'd put this film in the Expendables (franchise) when it comes to ratings which in our book is extremely lower or lower than the lowest. Sad to say I wasted money at the theater last night watching Jack Reacher: Never Go Back (2016). So in short, don't get your hopes up like I did; but for marketing those guys made this film look awesome so props to them.
