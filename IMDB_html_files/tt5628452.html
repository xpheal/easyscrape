The Stix Movie- Review
9 October 2016 | by dmwaite-94468 (Naples, Florida) – See all my reviews

I myself have watched Yoshi Player for quite a long time. His STIX Show is my favorite. When I found out The STIX Movie was coming out, I was really excited. When I saw the trailer it looked OK, but about a month ago, Yoshi Player released like, a "First Look" or "First 5 Minutes" or something like that. That footage seemed really funny and interesting. While the STIX Movie has a very short run time, of 42 Minutes, It's great how this was animated from just one guy. It's funny, clever, unique, and reminds me a lot pf South Park. I'll give The STIX Movie an 8.5/10. Check it out, it's worth your time! I honestly think this is really funny. Props to Yoshi Player for making such a good YouTube movie (Ahem, Lazer Team)
