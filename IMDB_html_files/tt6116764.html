Storyline

Ewan McGregor discusses his directorial debut film "American Pastoral", and the difficulties of acting and directing your own film. Sam Neill talks about his new movie "Hunt for the Wilder People" and his interaction with Princess Diana at the original premiere of "Jurassic Park". Miranda Hart has written a new book "Peggy and Me", a book about her relationship with her dog, and her various theories about people and their pets. Comedian John Bishop has a new tour starting in 2017. Danny DeVito discusses his new film "Curmudgeons", that he created with friends and family members.
